Grocery Tax Rebate

Food Assistance

The Grocery Rebate Tax is an annual cash payment to low-income individuals and families living in the City of Fort Collins and its Growth Management Area. It provides your family with direct assistance in exchange for the taxes you spend on food.

Spin Access

Discounted Spin Rentals

Spin offers e-bike and e-scooter as accessible and affordable ways to get around town. Through Spin Access, people with low income can receive a discount for Spin rentals. No smartphone? With Spin Access, you can rent a Spin bike or scooter with text messaging. No credit/debit card? Contact FC Moves at rruhlen@fcgov.com or (970) 416-2040 to purchase Spin Cash cards.
