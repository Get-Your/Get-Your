Choose Your Ride, Shift Your Ride!

Fort Collins received a grant from the Colorado Energy Office to provide income-qualified residents the opportunity to increase their transportation options by providing one of these: An e-bike and accessories necessary for riding, OR Membership for the Spin e-bike and e-scooter share service and accessories for riding. If you are interested in receiving an e-bike or Spin pass, complete the application by April 23, 2023.
