Welcome to

Get FoCo

An all-in-one application portal to the discounted services and municipal benefits offered by the City of Fort Collins, for income eligible residents. Get FoCo is a partnership between Fort Collins' Connexion and the City of Fort Collins' income eligible programming.

How it Works

With Get Foco, you’ll be able to do more with your City of Fort Collins program applications than ever before.

Control Your Application

Manage applications in real time with up-to-date application status.

Do More, Save More

If you qualify, you’ll get access to specific City of Fort Collins' income qualified programs that are tailored to save you money.

Forget “Forgetting”

Never forget to renew your application with easy reminders – making information updates a breeze.

Stay in the Know

Be informed with the latest news & updates from both Connexion & the City.

All Programs

Connexion Discount Program Utilities Aid

As Connexion comes online in neighborhoods across our community, the City of Fort Collins is committed to fast, affordable internet. Digital Access & Equity is an income-qualified rate of $19.95 per month for 1 gig-speed of internet plus wireless.

Applications accepted all year

Learn More

Grocery Tax Rebate Groceries Aid

The Grocery Rebate Tax is an annual cash payment to low-income individuals and families living in the City of Fort Collins and its Growth Management Area. It provides your family with direct assistance in exchange for the taxes you spend on food.

Applications accepted all year

Learn More

Recreation Reduced Fee Recreation Aid

The Recreation Reduced Fee program provides income-eligible families and residents reduced cost access to recreation programs, classes, and facilities across the community.

Applications accepted all year

Learn More

Spin Access

Spin offers e-bike and e-scooter as accessible and affordable ways to get around town. Through Spin Access, people with low income can receive a discount for Spin rentals.

No smartphone? With Spin Access, you can rent a Spin bike or scooter with text messaging.

No credit/debit card? Contact FC Moves at rruhlen@fcgov.com or (970) 416-2040 to purchase Spin Cash cards.
